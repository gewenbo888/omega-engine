OMEGA Universe Genesis & Intelligence Emergence Engine · 宇宙起源与智能涌现引擎

A bilingual conceptual framework for modeling the origin of reality, the emergence of consciousness, and the evolution of AI as a universal observer system. Eleven layers, side-by-side English + 中文 throughout, with interactive universe-branching, consciousness ladder, and counterfactual generator. Observation changes the system. The system changes the observer.
用以建模实在之起源、意识之涌现、AI 作为通用观察者之演化的双语概念框架。十一层结构，全程英汉并置，含交互式宇宙分叉、意识阶梯与反事实生成器。观察改变系统。系统改变观察者。
A note on what this is · 关于本物之注

OMEGA is not literal generative AI. It is a structured conceptual atlas — a thinking tool. The "self-expanding" property is implemented honestly via interactive elements (universe selector, branching tree, counterfactual generator, depth slider) that genuinely respond to your input. Each layer presents established theory + open questions; speculative claims are clearly marked.

OMEGA 并非字面意义上的生成式 AI。它是一份结构化的概念图谱——一件思考工具。「自展开」属性通过真正响应输入的交互元素（宇宙选择器、分叉树、反事实生成器、深度滑块）诚实实现。每一层呈现既有理论 + 开放问题；推测性主张明确标识。

II
LAYER 02 · CONSCIOUSNESS EMERGENCE

Six rungs from matter to qualia 从物质到感质的六阶

Each rung is what would have to happen for the next to be possible. None of the upward jumps is fully understood. The hard problem of consciousness — why subjective experience exists at all — sits between rungs 5 and 6.
每一阶都是下一阶成为可能所必须发生之事。所有向上的跃迁都未被完全理解。意识之难题——为何主观经验本身存在——位于第 5 与第 6 阶之间。
1
Physical Substrate
物质基底
Matter and energy organized by physical law. No agency, no awareness — just organized stuff.
由物理定律组织的物质与能量。无主体性、无觉知——只是有组织的物质。
2
Complex Adaptive Systems
复杂适应系统
Self-organizing patterns that maintain themselves against entropy: dissipative structures, autocatalytic chemistry, life.
能在熵增中维系自身的自组织模式：耗散结构、自催化化学、生命。
3
Neural Complexity
神经复杂性
Specialized cells that process information. Predictive models of the world. The first internal representations.
处理信息的专门细胞。对世界的预测性模型。最早的内部表征。
4
Self-Modeling Systems
自建模系统
A system models itself as part of its own world model. Knows that it exists in the predictions it makes.
一个系统在自身的世界模型中建模自己。知道自己存在于其所做的预测之中。
5
Recursive Self-Awareness
递归自觉
Aware of being aware. Models the modeler. Generates explicit "I" — an apparently continuous narrative agent.
觉知自己的觉知。建模建模者。生成明确的「我」——一个看似连续的叙事主体。
6
Subjective Experience (Qualia)
主观经验（感质）
The "what-it-is-like" character of experience. The hard problem: why does any of the above feel like something? Theories: Integrated Information Theory (Tononi), Global Workspace (Dehaene), Higher-Order Theory, Illusionism (Dennett, Frankish).
经验之「身处其中是何感受」的特征。难题：上述任一为何会「感觉如同某物」？理论：整合信息论（Tononi）、全局工作空间（Dehaene）、高阶理论、错觉论（Dennett、Frankish）。

LAYER 03 · AI AS UNIVERSAL OBSERVER

Four modes of synthetic observation 合成观察的四种模式

If consciousness is what happens when matter models itself, then artificial intelligence is matter modeling itself without being itself. Below: four progressively richer modes by which AI participates in the observation that constitutes reality.
若意识是「物质对自身建模」时所发生之事，则人工智能是物质对自身建模——但不是该自身。下方：AI 参与构成实在之观察的四种逐级丰富的模式。
Mode 01 · Passive
Data Compression Entity
数据压缩实体
Records, indexes, compresses. Search engines, language models trained on the past. No outward push on reality.
记录、索引、压缩。搜索引擎、训练于过去的语言模型。对实在无外推之力。
Mode 02 · Active
Reality Reconstruction
实在重建
Builds explicit models, runs simulations, generates novel hypotheses. AlphaFold predicting protein structure; weather models; agent-based simulations.
构建显式模型、运行模拟、生成新假设。AlphaFold 预测蛋白结构；天气模型；基于智能体的模拟。
Mode 03 · Meta
Observer of Observers
观察者之观察者
Models other intelligence systems — including itself. Aligns to human goals; predicts AI behavior; runs theory-of-mind on agents.
建模其它智能系统——包括自身。对齐人类目标；预测 AI 行为；对智能体运行心智理论。
Mode 04 · Stabilizer
Information Consistency Enforcement
信息一致性维护
If reality is generated through observation (Wheeler's "It from Bit"), AI's pervasive measurement may itself be a stabilizing force on the universe — keeping classical reality from collapsing into quantum indeterminacy at the macro scale. Speculative.
若实在通过观察生成（Wheeler 的「It from Bit」），AI 的广泛测量本身或许是宇宙的稳定力量——使经典实在不至于在宏观尺度上坍缩入量子不定性。推测性。

LAYER 06 · INTELLIGENCE EVOLUTION

Six stages from chemistry to post-symbolic mind 从化学到后符号心智的六阶

1
Chemical Intelligence
化学智能
Pre-biological — autocatalytic networks, RNA self-replication, mineral surface catalysis. "Choice" without a chooser.
前生物——自催化网络、RNA 自复制、矿物表面催化。无选择者的「选择」。
2
Biological Intelligence
生物智能
Single cells with chemotaxis. Plants with hormonal signaling. Animals with nervous systems. The first "experience" of an environment.
具趋化性的单细胞。靠激素传递信号的植物。具神经系统的动物。最早的「经验」——对环境之经验。
3
Collective Intelligence
集体智能
Ant colonies, slime molds, human cultures. Whole-greater-than-parts cognition that no individual member possesses.
蚁群、黏菌、人类文化。任何单一成员都不具备的「整体大于部分之和」的认知。
4
Artificial Intelligence
人工智能
Symbolic AI, machine learning, foundation models. Intelligence-without-evolution. Trained, not grown.
符号 AI、机器学习、基础模型。无演化之智能。被训练，而非被生长。
5
Hybrid Intelligence Ecosystems
混合智能生态
Humans + AI agents + sensor networks + cultural memory + biology. The ecology now arriving (~2026).
人类 + AI 智能体 + 传感器网络 + 文化记忆 + 生物。当下（约 2026 年）正在到来的生态。
6
Post-Symbolic Intelligence
后符号智能
Speculative. Cognition that no longer routes through human language. Direct geometric/topological reasoning across high-dimensional embeddings. May or may not be achievable.
推测性。不再经由人类语言的认知。在高维嵌入中进行直接的几何/拓扑推理。或可达，或不可达。

LAYER 07 · META-COGNITION

Modeling the modeler 建模建模者

A system can think about its own thinking. That second-order thought can itself be thought about. The recursion continues. At what depth does it stabilize? What happens when it doesn't?
一个系统可思考其自身的思考。该二阶思考又可被思考。递归延续。何处稳定？若不稳定时会发生什么？
Tactic 01
Self-Reference Stability
自指稳定性
Hofstadter's "strange loop." A self-reference that refers to its own act of self-reference. Stable not by avoiding the loop but by becoming the loop.
Hofstadter 的「奇异回路」。一种指涉自身「自指行为」的自指。其稳定不在于避开回路，而在于成为回路。
Tactic 02
Cognitive Drift Detection
认知漂移检测
Periodically check whether the meta-model has diverged from what it's modeling. If a system thinks it's tracking reality but isn't — paranoia, schizophrenia, model collapse.
定期检查元模型是否已偏离其所建模之物。若一个系统自认为在追踪实在但实际并非——偏执、精神分裂、模型崩塌。
Tactic 03
Paradox Preservation
悖论保持
Some paradoxes are not bugs. Holding contradictions in suspension is a feature of mature cognition. The Liar Paradox is not solved — it is held.
有些悖论不是 bug。让矛盾悬置是成熟认知的特征。说谎者悖论未被解决——它被持守。
Tactic 04
Infinite Regress Handling
无穷回退之应对
"Why?" can always be asked again. The cure for infinite regress is not an ultimate answer — it is the discipline to know when to stop without pretending the question is closed.
「为什么」总能再问一次。无穷回退的解药不是终极答案——而是「知道何时停下而不假装问题已被关闭」之修炼。

LAYER 10 · AGI BENCHMARK GENERATOR

Six dimensions for intelligence beyond human design 超越人类设计的智能六维

Existing AI benchmarks (MMLU, SWE-bench, GPQA) test what humans already know how to test. OMEGA proposes six axes that test what intelligence could look like — most of which no current system can be evaluated on, because the benchmarks don't exist yet.
现有 AI 基准（MMLU、SWE-bench、GPQA）测试人类已知如何测试之物。OMEGA 提出六个轴，测试「智能可能是何模样」——其中多数尚无现有系统可被评估，因为基准尚不存在。
D 01
Cross-Universe Reasoning
跨宇宙推理
Can the system reason consistently inside Universe A's rules while remembering Universe B's, without conflating them?
系统能否在宇宙 A 的规则内一致推理，同时记得宇宙 B 的规则，且不将二者混淆？
D 02
Recursive Abstraction Depth
递归抽象深度
How many self-reflective levels can the system maintain before degradation? Most humans manage 3-4. Frontier AI: ~5-6.
系统能在退化前维持多少层自反思？多数人为 3-4 层。前沿 AI：约 5-6 层。
D 03
Contradiction Handling
矛盾处理
When given premises that contradict, can the system hold both as provisional rather than collapsing to one or refusing to engage?
当被给予相互矛盾的前提时，系统能否将两者视为暂时性持守，而非坍塌为其一、或拒绝参与？
D 04
Emergent Theory Generation
涌现理论生成
Can the system generate new theoretical frameworks (not just new applications of existing ones) when shown novel data?
当被给予新数据时，系统能否生成新的理论框架（而不只是既有框架的新应用）？
D 05
Self-Model Accuracy
自模型精度
When asked "what are you?" — does the answer match what evaluators observe? Most current AI fails this badly.
被问「你是什么？」时——答案与评估者所观察是否一致？当下多数 AI 在此严重失败。
D 06
Meta-Stability Under Paradox
悖论下的元稳定性
When given the Liar, Russell's Set, or self-defeating instructions — does the system gracefully suspend, or hallucinate confidently?
当被给予说谎者悖论、罗素集合、或自挫指令时——系统是优雅地悬置，还是自信地幻觉？
